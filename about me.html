A little about my self...I worked most of my adult life in printing houses, mostly as a graphic designer. In recent years I have worked in a factory for the production of packaging - packaging printing. My job was to oversee the production. I love seeing how things are created, the process that produces packaging from scratch. I understood and saw the need for further testing and quality assurance before final production. I believe and hope that I will succeed in line in QA.

I recently started a QA course at the College of Automation, with Gal Matalon and Kobi Yunsey. I am waiting to prove myself as a QA in a successful company, to contribute to the success of the company.
